• Research to determine the opinion of

the public in Gampaha District

6:49 AM Technology

The research was conducted parallel to the 16th Sri Lankan Parliamentary Election that was held on 05th August 2020. Amidst the influence of the Corona pandemic that highly affected the lifestyle of the public, it appeared that certain socio-political and economic tendencies have resulted in significant changes in the responses of the public during the pre-election times. This research aimed to realize such varieties of public responses properly and to position the political parties to organize their campaigns systematically. This research further investigated the media usage of the public in Gampaha district to measure the popularity of the media channels. The research is a descriptive portrayal of the age groups, gender, occupation, monthly income, and the general opinion of the public in Gampaha district. Both quantitative and qualitative research methods were used while giving prominence to quantitative data during the process of analyzing. Samples were selected covering the whole district. Data collection and analysis were done systematically adhering to the research methodologies.


• Research to determine the customer preference on House purchase

The behavior of a customer basically rests upon several factors when purchasing a house. Such factors mainly depend on the attitudes and perceptions of customers with distinct tastes. Accordingly, the nature of the house the customer tends to buy directly depends on the professional status, income, and the attitudes of the customer. Therefore, identifying the varied behavior of the customer makes it possible for a company to study the preferences of the customer before the construction of the house and conveniently promote them to the market. The objective of this research is to study and investigate the factors that affect a customer to purchase a newly built house. This research moreover investigates the make and the arrangement of the house that consists of the plan, appearance, basic components of the surrounding, and essential facilities. Further, this research holds the objective of reviewing the media companies and websites of which the advertisements are referred by the customers before purchasing a house. Computer-Assisted Web Interviewing (CAWI) and Focus group discussions were used to gather quantitative and qualitative data respectively.
